Gluten-Free Dinner Recipes

Gluten-Free Chicken Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • Mixed vegetables (broccoli, carrots, bell peppers)
  • Gluten-free soy sauce
  • Rice vinegar
  • Sesame oil
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Gluten-free cornstarch

Instructions:

  1. Stir-fry chicken and vegetables in a wok or large skillet.
  2. Add soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, ginger, and garlic.
  3. Toss to coat and cook until chicken is cooked through.
  4. Thicken sauce with cornstarch slurry.
  5. Serve over gluten-free rice or quinoa.

Gluten-Free Salmon with Roasted Asparagus

Ingredients:

  • Salmon fillets
  • Asparagus spears
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Toss asparagus with olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Roast asparagus until tender-crisp.
  4. Season salmon with salt and pepper.
  5. Grill or bake salmon until cooked through.
  6. Serve salmon with roasted asparagus.

Gluten-Free Desserts

Gluten-Free Peanut Butter Cookies

Ingredients:

  • Gluten-free all-purpose flour
  • Almond flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Butter, softened
  • Brown sugar
  • Peanut butter
  • Egg
  • Vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, almond flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, cream together butter, brown sugar, peanut butter, egg, and vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually add dry ingredients to wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  5. Drop by rounded tablespoons onto ungreased baking sheet.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until golden brown.

Gluten-Free Banana Bread

Ingredients:

  • Gluten-free all-purpose flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Ripe bananas, mashed
  • Egg
  • Butter, melted
  • Brown sugar
  • Vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine mashed bananas, egg, melted butter, brown sugar, and vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually add dry ingredients to wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  5. Pour into a greased loaf pan.
  6. Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

Gluten-Free Pancake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Gluten-free pancake mix
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Butter, melted

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine gluten-free pancake mix, milk, egg, and melted butter.
  2. Stir until just combined.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  4. Pour batter onto hot skillet.
  5. Cook until bubbles form on the surface and edges are set.
  6. Flip and cook until golden brown on the other side.

Gluten-Free Cake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Gluten-free cake mix
  • Water
  • Oil
  • Eggs
  • Frosting of your choice

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix gluten-free cake mix with water, oil, and eggs according to package instructions.
  3. Pour batter into a greased cake pan.
  4. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  5. Let cake cool before frosting.
